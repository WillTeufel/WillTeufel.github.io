OF MICE AND MEN

Of Mice and Men, is an American metalcore band from Orange County, California. The group was founded by Austin Carlile and Jaxin Hall in mid-2009 after Carlile's departure from his old band Attack Attack. Since 2009, the band has released five studio albums. This band has kept a heavy metalcore sound since the beginning and has become an icon of the genre. The band took off in 2010 with their album "The Flood" featuring the song "Ohioisonfire" which topped the metal charts for the entire year following
